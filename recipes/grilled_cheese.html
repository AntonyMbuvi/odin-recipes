HOW TO COOK a Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Making a Grilled Cheese Sandwich can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Grilled Cheese Sandwich Ingredients

  • Best Cheese for Grilled Cheese: This recipe calls for sliced Cheddar, which is a wonderfully crowd-pleasing cheese. You can use sharp Cheddar, mild Cheddar, or a blend of both. You can really make grilled cheese with any type of bread you like, but you should make sure the loaf is sturdy enough to handle the heat. Thinly sliced, delicate breads will quickly fall apart.

    2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up)
    3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
    4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.

How to Make Grilled Cheese Sandwich Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade grilled cheese sandwich:

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
